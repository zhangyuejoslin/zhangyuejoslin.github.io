Yue Zhang

Hello! I am a Ph.D. at Michigan State University, supervised by Prof. Parisa Kordjamshidi. I was a visiting scholar at Virginia Tech, working with Prof. Lifu Huang. Prior to that, I obtained my master degree from Peking University. My research interests include multimodal learning, embodied agent, and spatial reasoning.
